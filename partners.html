Storj Partner Program

Storj Labs is dedicated to helping our partners build solutions together.

The Storj Partner Program is for companies that want to work with us to build solutions, create value for customers, and drive adoption and use of distributed storage.

The Storj Partner Program recruits, on-boards, and drives mutually profitable long-term partnerships which generate incremental subscription revenue. The Storj Platform delivers unique value for Partners:

Economical

By leveraging the vast amount of unused storage capacity already connected to the Internet and not investing in costly data centers, distributed cloud storage is inherently more cost effective and those cost savings are passed on to our partners.

Reliable

Distributed, broadly decentralized storage is architected to be inherently more reliable and resilient than centralized storage.

Secure

With client-side encryption and erasure coding by default, and small pieces of files spread over a globally distributed network of storage nodes, storing files on Storj is like spreading encrypted sand on an encrypted beach.

Performant

With multi-threaded concurrent downloads of file pieces, distributed storage is faster and more efficient with bandwidth consumption than traditional cloud storage, whether itâ€™s streaming specific ranges within segments of files or downloading entire files.

Private

Client-side end-to-end encryption and zero-knowledge/zero trust storage guarantee that end users are in complete control of their data privacy.

Open Source

Open Source. All of the software components of the Storj Platform are completely open-source. With an active community of over 50,000 developers building and innovating on the platform, and dozens of members actively contributing to the source, Storj's commitment to open-source ensures the highest level of security, quality, auditability, and, of course, freedom.

Partnership Types

The programs offer maximum flexibility for partners to decide
where to focus and how to best optimize their investments.

Technology Partners

A technology partnership with Storj is for those companies that are integrating Storj as a storage layer, or developing products that will white-label Storj Platform storage. Additionally, technology partners include companies interested in a Value Added Reseller or Systems Integrator relationship. Storj offers wholesale pricing with tiered discounts based on total storage and bandwidth use.

Open-Source Partners

Storj has developed the Open-Source Partner Program to give Open-Source Partners an opportunity to generate revenue by incorporating Storj as a storage layer into their application. Open-Source applications that have a need for embedded storage but rely on end users to procure third-party cloud storage can benefit from revenue sharing when Storj is selected for cloud storage.
